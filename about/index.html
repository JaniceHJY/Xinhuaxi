About Xinhuaxi

Xinhuaxi is a one-stop OEM & ODM partner for wellness and personal care brands seeking reliable, scalable manufacturing solutions in China.

Backed by an integrated industrial park with multiple certified factories, we coordinate sourcing, formulation development, production, packaging, and supply chain management under one unified system. This structure allows us to offer flexibility, consistent quality control, and efficient turnaround times across multiple product categories.

From proprietary ingredient innovation to finished goods manufacturing, we help brands reduce development gaps, accelerate market entry, and build differentiated product portfolios.

We are not just a manufacturer.

We are a strategic supply partner.
